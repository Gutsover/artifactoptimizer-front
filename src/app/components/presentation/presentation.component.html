ArtifactOptimizer for Genshin Impact

Welcome to the tool ArtifactOptimizer!

For whom ?

This tool is for experienced Genshin Impact players who own a lot of rare artifacts with 4 and 5 stars value.

Why ?

When our inventory overflows, it becomes difficult to equip our characters with the most suitable artifacts to increase the desired characteristics.

How ?

ArtifactOptimizer allows you to save in your collection the artifacts you will collect over time. You will be able to select your character and define the set or sets of artifacts you want and choose the primary and secondary stats you want to prioritize for each piece of artifact.

ArtifactOptimizer will search for you the 5 ultimate artifacts for your hero according to your criteria.

You will be able to save the results in your collection so that the artifacts are not reused for other characters.
